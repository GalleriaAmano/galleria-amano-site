Flowers have been my muse as of lately.
Something about them has been catching my attention. Not much so how they look or smell… but how they exist. How uniquely individual they are. How each are a testimony to living in true purpose no matter what shade, texture, hue, or type of flower they are. Maybe that's why they were always meant to be given.

Like an acknowledgement of witnessing.
A celebration for existence.
A moment to share.
